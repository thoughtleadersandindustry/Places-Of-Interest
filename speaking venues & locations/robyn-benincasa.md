# Robyn Benincasa
**Category:** Leadership, Resilience, Team Culture | **Origin:** United States (Colorado-based)

## Profile
Award-winning keynote speaker, former firefighter, world champion adventure racer, and founder of Project Athena Foundation. With 20+ years of endurance sports experience, Robyn combines authentic stories of grit, resilience, and high-pressure teamwork to inspire leaders and organizations globally.

## Core Speaking Topics
- **Team Culture & Cohesion:** Building psychological safety and trust under pressure
- **Leadership in Crisis:** Decision-making in high-stakes environments
- **Resilience & Adversity:** Transforming setbacks into competitive advantages
- **Peak Performance:** Individual and organizational performance optimization

## Professional Background
- **Former Firefighter:** Emergency response, crisis management, rapid team coordination
- **World Champion Adventure Racer:** Guinness World Record holder; 10x Ironman finisher
- **Author:** *How Winning Works: 8 Essential Leadership Lessons From The Toughest Teams On Earth*
- **Founder:** Project Athena Foundation — helping survivors transform life setbacks through team-based endurance adventures

## Speaking Style
Robyn emphasizes authentic storytelling, human connection, and lasting cultural impact. Her keynotes are grounded in real experience rather than theoretical frameworks, making them highly applicable to technical teams, executive leaders, and cross-functional organizations.
